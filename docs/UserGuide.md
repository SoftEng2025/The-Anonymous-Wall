# User Guide

## Getting Started

### 1. Accessing the App
Open the application in your web browser. You will land on the **Home Page**, which features a live typing animation and quick access buttons.

### 2. Logging In
You can browse content without logging in, but to post or like, you need an account.
1. Click the **"Login"** button in the top right corner.
2. Choose your method:
    - **Continue with Google**: Uses your Google account for a permanent profile.
    - **Continue as Guest**: Creates a temporary anonymous session.

## Using the Freedom Wall

### Viewing Messages
1. Click **"Browse Wall"** or **"Freedom Wall"** in the navigation.
2. You will see a grid of colorful message cards.
3. Use the **Search Bar** to find messages for a specific person (e.g., "To: Sarah").

### Posting a Message
1. Click the **"Create Post"** button (or the "+" icon).
2. **Recipient**: Enter the name of the person you are writing to (or "Everyone").
3. **Message**: Write your short message (max 95 characters).
4. **Theme**: Select a color card to style your message.
5. **Mood**: Pick an icon that represents your feeling.
6. Click **"Send"**. Your message will appear on the wall instantly!

## Using the Forum

### Browsing Boards
1. Click **"Forum"** in the navigation.
2. You will see a list of **Boards** (categories) like "General", "Love", "Rant".
3. Click a board to see posts within that topic.

### Creating a Discussion
1. Click the **"Create Post"** button.
2. **Title**: Give your post a catchy title.
3. **Board**: Select the appropriate category.
4. **Content**: Write your full story or question.
5. Click **"Post"**.

### Interacting
- **Like**: Click the heart icon to show support.
- **Reply**: Click the comment icon to view the discussion and add your own reply.
- **Save**: Click the bookmark icon to save the post to your profile.

## Managing Your Profile
1. Click your **Avatar** in the top right.
2. **Edit Username**: Click the pencil icon next to your name to change it.
3. **Regenerate Identity**: Click the refresh icon on your avatar to get a new look. **Note**: This updates your avatar on ALL your past posts too!
4. **History**: View your "My Posts" and "Saved Posts" tabs.
